About

OsmPoint is a simple tool, written mainly in Python, that helps you create and explore points of interest from OpenStreetMap.

On the homepage you can browse through the bars, coffee shops, restaurants, nightclubs, theaters and gas stations in Bucharest.

To add a POI, just let your GPS track your current position, mark the exact spot, enter a name and a type of location, and send it. An admin will approve it in no time and submit it to OpenStreetMap.
